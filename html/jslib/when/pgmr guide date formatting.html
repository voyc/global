Inch could be cm maybe. a Year is a unit of 1 rotation around the sun. A second is not a division Both the geographic map and the time map are based on a circle. The time map is based on three circles: the revolution around the sun, the rotation of the earth, and the clock face. On a geographic map a second is a division of the total circle, and varies in length depending on latitude. On a time map, the second retains a constant length, and the number of seconds in the circle vary as necessary. Another difference between geographic and time maps: the time map has a much longer scale. Earth geography is confined to 360 degrees in each dimension. Entering dates.
